# Sprint Retrospective

This week we conducted our sprint retrospective, we went over and reviewed our last sprint week looking over the morning meeting documents and assesing what we completed,
what we didn't and why and when things went wrong. We noted down key points and put them into the 5 L's assessing what we Liked, learned, lacked and longed for. We noted
these down on a whiteboard and went on to use these points to help plan out our next step, the action plan.

Once we realised where things went wrong and why, we created a document in which we clarified what exactly we needed to improve on and how we would actually do it.
We disscussed points for this document as a team and ensured it was a group discussion so that we could get multiple view points and ensure everyone in the team felt 
listend too and heard.
