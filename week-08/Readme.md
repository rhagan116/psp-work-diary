# Week 8 SLEP

In week 8 we discussed the Social, Legal, Ethical and Professional Issues we might face in our project, when looking into UI/UX ethics and SLEP issues we did notice that our project was exempt from many of these 
issues. We don't interact with users, we dont have a UI and we don't store any data. However there were some things that we found we need to be wary of.

Legal Issues:
There is the ability to modify the cars liveries as well as some garage doors and signs, using a brand's logo or someones artwork on our car without their permission or under fair use could breach both the Trade Marks
Act 1994 and the Copyright, Designs and Patents Act 1998. As a result we will only use art we have made or art that is licensed under Creative Commons.
On top of images any code we might find online and use/modify in our own code must be credited as the code we implement could be under similar legal acts.

Social/Ethical:
We must make a walkthrough presentation/guide for our racebot, when making this we must ensure that the walkthrough is accsesible and easy to understand. We will ensure that our guide can be followed by people of all
skills/abilites as well as ensure that any documents we make are displayed clearly and aren't difficult to read physically (font colours and font sizing for example).
